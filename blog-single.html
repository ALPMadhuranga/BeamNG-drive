WHAT IS BEAMNG.DRIVE AND HOW TO PLAY?

  • Benjamin
  • Feb 18, 2023
  • 12 Comments

BeamNG.drive is about competition. It's about testing yourself against the best drivers in the world and coming out on top. It's about winning and being the best. BeamNG.drive is about all of these things and more. It's a game that has something for everyone. So whether you're a casual player or a hardcore sim racer, there's something in BeamNG.drive for you.

BeamNG.drive is a realistic, immersive driving game offering near-limitless possibilities. Our soft-body physics engine simulates every vehicle component in real-time, resulting in dynamic and realistic behavior. The driving feel is authentic and visceral, and crashes are realistic and violent, yet the physics are accessible enough to drive with a keyboard or gamepad while still being authentic. This is a game where players are encouraged to experiment, explore, and discover.

BeamNG.drive is a soft-body physics vehicle simulator featuring unique vehicle destruction powered by a sophisticated soft-body physics engine. All vehicles in BeamNG.drive are soft-body physics objects, which means they are modeled after real-life vehicles and react realistically to collisions, damage, and other forces. This allows for a unique and realistic driving and crashing experience.
